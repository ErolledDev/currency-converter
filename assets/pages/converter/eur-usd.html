Understanding EUR to USD Exchange Rate

The EUR/USD exchange rate represents how many US Dollars (USD) can be bought with one Euro (EUR). This currency pair, often called "Fiber," is the most actively traded pair in the forex market.

Key Points

  • World's most liquid currency pair
  • Represents two largest economies
  • Key benchmark for global trade
  • Major reserve currencies
