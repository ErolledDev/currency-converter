Understanding CNY to HKD Exchange Rate

The CNY/HKD exchange rate shows how many Hong Kong Dollars (HKD) can be purchased with one Chinese Yuan (CNY). This currency pair reflects the close economic ties between mainland China and Hong Kong, with significant implications for regional trade and investment flows.
