Understanding USD to ILS Exchange Rate

The USD/ILS exchange rate shows how many Israeli Shekels (ILS) can be purchased with one US Dollar (USD). This currency pair reflects Israel's strong technology sector and its position as a major innovation hub in the Middle East.

Key Points

  • Middle Eastern tech hub currency
  • BOI monetary policy
  • High-tech sector influence
  • Regional economic stability
