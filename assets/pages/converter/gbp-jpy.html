Understanding GBP to JPY Exchange Rate

The GBP/JPY exchange rate shows how many Japanese Yen (JPY) can be purchased with one British Pound (GBP). This currency pair, often called the "Dragon," is known for its volatility and is influenced by both UK and Japanese economic factors.

Key Points

  • Major cross-rate pair
  • High volatility
  • Interest rate differential
  • Risk sentiment indicator
