Understanding EUR to RON Exchange Rate

The EUR/RON exchange rate represents how many Romanian Leu (RON) can be purchased with one Euro (EUR). This currency pair is important for Romania's trade with the EU and reflects the country's progress toward eventual Euro adoption.

Key Points

  • Eastern European currency pair
  • BNR monetary policy
  • EU convergence criteria
  • Regional economic integration
