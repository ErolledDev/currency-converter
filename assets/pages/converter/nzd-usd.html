Understanding NZD to USD Exchange Rate

The NZD/USD exchange rate shows how many US Dollars (USD) can be purchased with one New Zealand Dollar (NZD). This currency pair, known as the "Kiwi," is influenced by dairy prices, risk sentiment, and New Zealand's agricultural exports.

Key Points

  • Agricultural commodity currency
  • Sensitive to dairy prices
  • Influenced by risk sentiment
  • Key for Asia-Pacific trade
