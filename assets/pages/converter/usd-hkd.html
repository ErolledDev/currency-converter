Understanding USD to HKD Exchange Rate

The USD/HKD exchange rate shows how many Hong Kong Dollars (HKD) can be purchased with one US Dollar (USD). The Hong Kong Dollar is pegged to the US Dollar through a currency board system, maintaining stability in the exchange rate.

Key Points

  • Currency board system
  • Linked exchange rate
  • Financial center currency
  • Trading hub importance
