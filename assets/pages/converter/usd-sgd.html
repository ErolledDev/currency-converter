Understanding USD to SGD Exchange Rate

The USD/SGD exchange rate shows how many Singapore Dollars (SGD) can be purchased with one US Dollar (USD). The Singapore Dollar is a major Asian currency and reflects Singapore's role as a key financial hub in the Asia-Pacific region.

Key Points

  • Major Asian financial center currency
  • MAS monetary policy influence
  • Trade-weighted exchange rate
  • Regional economic indicator
