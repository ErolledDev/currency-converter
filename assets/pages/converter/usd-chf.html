Understanding USD to CHF Exchange Rate

The USD/CHF exchange rate shows how many Swiss Francs (CHF) can be purchased with one US Dollar (USD). The Swiss Franc is known as a safe-haven currency, often strengthening during times of global economic uncertainty.

Key Points

  • Major safe-haven currency pair
  • Influenced by global risk sentiment
  • Swiss National Bank monetary policy
  • Important for international trade
