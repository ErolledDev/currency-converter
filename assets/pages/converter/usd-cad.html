Understanding USD to CAD Exchange Rate

The USD/CAD exchange rate shows how many Canadian Dollars (CAD) can be purchased with one US Dollar (USD). Often called the "loonie," this currency pair is heavily influenced by commodity prices, particularly oil, and the strong trade relationship between the United States and Canada.

Key Points

  • Major North American currency pair
  • Oil price sensitivity
  • USMCA trade impact
  • Commodity currency
