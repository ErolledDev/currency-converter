Currency Glossary

Understanding currency exchange terminology

  • A
  • B
  • C

A

Ask Price
The price at which a currency can be purchased. Also known as the offer price.
Arbitrage
The simultaneous buying and selling of currencies in different markets to profit from price differences.

B

Base Currency
The first currency quoted in a currency pair. For example, in EUR/USD, EUR is the base currency.
Bid Price
The price at which a currency can be sold.

C

Currency Pair
The quotation of two different currencies, with the value of one currency being quoted against the other.
Cross Rate
An exchange rate between two currencies, both of which are not the official currency of the country in which the exchange rate quote is given.
